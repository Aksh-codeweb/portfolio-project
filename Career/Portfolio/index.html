My Development Journey

Here's the story of my path to becoming a web developer, with its challenges and triumphs.

2019 - Inspiration

Inspired by My Sister

When I was in 10th standard, I looked at my sister, who is also an engineer, and saw her pursuing development. Her passion for creating digital solutions inspired me to explore the world of technology. Watching her build applications and solve complex problems ignited my curiosity and set me on this path.

2020 - Science Path

Chose Science Stream

Inspired by my sister's journey, I decided to take the science stream in college. I was fascinated by how technology could solve real-world problems and wanted to build a strong foundation in logical thinking and problem-solving. This decision marked the beginning of my formal journey into the world of technology and innovation.

2021 - JEE Preparation

Prepared for JEE Exams

I dedicated myself to preparing for the JEE exams, studying physics, chemistry, and mathematics with determination. This period taught me discipline, perseverance, and the importance of consistent effort. Though challenging, this experience strengthened my problem-solving abilities and my resolve to pursue a career in technology.

2022 - Setback

Didn't Clear JEE

Despite my efforts, I didn't clear the JEE exam and was unable to join a B.Tech program. This was a difficult period, but it taught me resilience and the importance of adapting to challenges. Rather than giving up, I began exploring alternative paths to achieve my dream of working in technology.

2022 - New Path

Pursued B.Sc. I.T.

I refused to let setbacks define my future and enrolled in a B.Sc. program in Information Technology. Here, I discovered my passion for web development and began learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This alternative path allowed me to focus specifically on the skills I needed to become a web developer.

2025 - Present

Creating Websites

Today, I'm actively building websites and expanding my skills in web development. Each project teaches me something new and brings me closer to my goal of becoming a professional developer. My journey has taught me that success isn't about the path you take, but the passion and persistence you bring to it.

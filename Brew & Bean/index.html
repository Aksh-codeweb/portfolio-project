Our Story

Brewing Excellence Since 2010

Brew & Bean India was founded with a simple mission: to bring together the rich coffee traditions of India with modern artisan techniques. Our beans are sourced from the finest estates in Chikmagalur, Coorg, and Araku Valley.

We're passionate about creating a space where community thrives over excellent coffee. Our baristas are trained in both traditional Indian brewing methods and contemporary coffee artistry.

Every cup tells a story of careful sourcing, precise roasting, and passionate brewing. Come be a part of our story.

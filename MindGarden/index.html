Art Activities

5-Minute Doodle Exercise

5 min

Let your creativity flow without judgment. Doodle whatever comes to mind for 5 minutes.

  1. Grab a pen and paper
  2. Set a timer for 5 minutes
  3. Start doodling without overthinking
  4. Notice how you feel afterward

Color Therapy

15 min

Use colors to express your current mood through abstract art.

  1. Choose colors that represent how you feel
  2. Create an abstract piece using those colors
  3. Don't worry about making it "look like" anything
  4. Reflect on why you chose those colors
